About Us

A kitchen built around one hearth, not a dozen appliances.

Ember & Oak opened with a simple idea: cook the way people cooked before electricity made it easy — over real wood, at the mercy of real heat.

Our entire kitchen runs on a custom oak-fired hearth. Vegetables, meats, even our cocktails pass near that fire before they reach your table, picking up a little smoke and a lot of character along the way.

We rewrite the menu with the seasons, because a good kitchen listens to what's actually growing rather than what's printed on last year's card. Expect char where you don't usually find it, and a dining room built for lingering.
